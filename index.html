The Premise

On a frigid evening in the middle of December, five students huddled around an aged computer monitor in the Harvard Computer Lab.

"It can't be..." one whispered.

"But it is!" another proclaimed.

"It's... gone," Leslie concluded glumly, hovering the mouse cursor over the page. "Someone has deleted my final project!"

"Are you sure you didn't delete it by accident?" Emmalee asked, placing a comforting hand on Leslie's shoulder.

"I'm sure," Leslie replied confidently. "I was working on it just this morning, and made sure to have a backup file. But for some reason, the backup file and the original have been completely overwritten."

Daniel's brows furrowed. "Overwritten, you say?"

"Yeah. All of the files are still there, but when I open them up-- there's this garbled nonsense instead of my actual work. It's strange."

“Someone’s idea of a prank, maybe?” Scott mused, plopping down into an empty chair.

Leslie, Daniel, Anna, Emmalee, and Scott

“Not a very good prank, if you ask me. But wait...” Anna frowned. “You were working on it this morning, then left to grab lunch and do some errands. Emmalee, you were on duty for watching the lab today, right? Did you see anyone suspicious come through?”

Emmalee closed her eyes briefly. “No, nobody suspicious has been here. In fact- I don’t think anyone outside of the five of us has been in here all day!”

Leslie glanced up from the monitor, shocked. “But that means that whoever did this is one of the five of us!” An uncomfortable silence hovered over the room, only to be broken by a sudden shout.

“Oh!” Scott exclaimed, causing everyone to look up. “Look- a note!” He peeled a sticky note off of the back of the monitor.

“If you want your project back, you’ll have to solve my mystery! I’ve replaced your project page with a puzzle you’ll have to solve. You might need help from a famous Roman to figure it out, though.”

“A puzzle,” Leslie groaned, “but I only have a few hours left until my project is due… how will I ever be able to submit it in time?”

Help Leslie figure out which of her four classmates overwrote her code by investigating the site that has taken its place.
